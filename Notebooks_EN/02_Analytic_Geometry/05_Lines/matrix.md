+The general equation of a sphere with center \((h, k, l)\) and radius \(r\) is:

$$
(x - h)^2 + (y - k)^2 + (z - l)^2 = r^2
$$

Given:
- Center \(P = (1, 2, 3)\), so \(h = 1\), \(k = 2\), and \(l = 3\),
- Radius \(r = 3\),

Substituting these values into the formula, we get:

$$
(x - 1)^2 + (y - 2)^2 + (z - 3)^2 = 3^2
$$

Simplifying:

$$
(x - 1)^2 + (y - 2)^2 + (z - 3)^2 = 9
$$
